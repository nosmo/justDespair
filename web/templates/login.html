JustDespair

JustDespair is a way to calculate your total JustEat purchase history. Every order, since you started using the service. Yes, you want to know.

JustDespair will also (once you have submitted your own entry) show you the highest entry total seen across all logins.

What you should know before using using this tool

  • Your JustEat login details are required to collect this information. These details are not stored by this app, but you submit them at your own risk.
  • By using this app, if your total sum is the highest historical value seen, you consent to having this information stored - completely anonymously. You can see this for yourself in the source code.
